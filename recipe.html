COLLINS RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Bread slices.
  • 2 cloves garlic-minced
  • 1 heaped tablespoon of blue band or butter
  • bunch of dhania - finely chopped
  • Grated cheddar or parmesan cheese.

Procedure

  • Mix the butter/blue band with the dhania and minced garlic until you get a smooth paste.
  • Spread this on each slice (one side only) then place these slices on a baking tray.
  • Sprinkle a generous amount of the cheese on top of the slices and place these on a preheated oven.
  • Bake for 6-8 minutes at 180 degrees.
  • The results should be a
  • scrumptious crunchy flavored bread.
  • Serve with your preferred beverage.
